This was another early leather-crafting project: a leather cover for the Toyota's keyfob.

I've also been doing a LOT more 3D printing lately, since I picked up a Bambu P2S to replace Creakity Max Neo I had before. This is a highly recommended upgrade. With the Creakity, just getting anything to print at all was a chore. With the Bambu, it's a pleasure.

Anyways, I've been enjoying printing so much I took a minute to print out a template in case I ever want to make another of these.
