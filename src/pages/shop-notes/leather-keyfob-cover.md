I've wanted to take up leather crafting for awhile. It's another one of those built-for-the-long-haul analog things that appeals when one spends their whole career in tech.

Because I've started from zero and I have no idea what I'm doing, most of my early results are not great, but I'm enjoying it and learning. 

This is one of those early efforts: a place to put all the leather punches and such that you need for this sort of work. I figured I need to learn how to saddle stitch better, and since it's only for myself, it's OK if it's not perfect.

  {% figure img_path "toolroll1.jpeg", "This was my first effort. It was not good." %}

After my first effort, I was almost immediately sure I could do better and ready to try again. So shortly after I made a second version using better leather, and a system to let it fold up on itself using snaps. Pretty nifty. And the stitching is better.
